# Legal Framework

## Jus Postulandi

Allows citizens to act without a lawyer in small claims (up to 20 minimum wages).

## Labor Context

Workers can file complaints directly in labor courts.

## Habeas Corpus

Can be filed by any citizen in case of unlawful restriction of freedom.

---

## Ethical Considerations

The platform provides informational and technological support, not legal representation.
